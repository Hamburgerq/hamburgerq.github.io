VolumeUp 1.0.0 release notes-2025.05.26

This is the first version of VolumeUp, which includes following functions:
1. Batch adjust audio volume.
2. Support input and output formats: WAV, MP3, FLAC, AIFF, AAC, OGG, MP4, MOV, MKV
3. Audio clipping prevention.
4. Basic customization options, such as dark theme, multiple languages(English, Traditional Chinese and Japanese), changable loudness unit(LUFS or dB), target volume memorization.
